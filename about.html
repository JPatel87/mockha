About us

Welcome to Mockha! We are husband and wife Judy and Mark Parker; the proud owners of Mockha

The inspiration behind Mockha sprouted from our three loves in life; coffee, socialising and all things sweet!

Mockha is a place where vegan food lovers can come together and catch up over coffee and cake without having to worry about what they can or can't have, because our menu is 100% vegan!

All our cakes are inspired frm traditional cake recipes which we have tried and tested over many years and our coffees are a blend of unique flavours inspired from our trips around Brazil and India

Come and try for yourself, we promosie you won't be disappointed!
